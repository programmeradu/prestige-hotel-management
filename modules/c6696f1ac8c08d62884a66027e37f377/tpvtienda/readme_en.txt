
REQUIREMENTS

This software is encrypted with Ioncube, and his server must enable this extension, it's a very common feature, and most of the hosting companys includes already in their basic services.


INSTALLATION 

In the file that you have received you can see a file called "tpvtienda.zip", this is the module. 
This file must be uploaded from the modules tab of his prestashop, after do clic on the "install" button of the POS Prestashop module.
Once it's installed, go to my account in www.pos-tpv.com in the "key license" button, ans insert your domain name. With the license generated, now you can insert the license in your store and ready!

We recommend take a look to the module configuration to check the options and enable or disable the options to customize the POS to your bussiness.
